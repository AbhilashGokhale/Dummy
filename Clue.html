Rashmi - The Maid

Story Line

Detective: Where were you, at the time of the murder?
Rashmi: I was at home with my kids.
Detective: Did you find anything strange during your work time in their house?
Rashmi: The couple were very friendly and quiet.
But Sir’s brother keeps troubling them due to which they get disturbed a lot.
Detective: Do you know what the arguments are all about?
Rashmi: No idea Sir. But I think it is because of an ancestral property which I heard Madam and Sir
arguing about.



Detective: Anyone else suspicious who is acquainted with the couple?
Rashmi: I noticed Madam’s colleague come by whom Madam doesn’t like much.
Detective: How do you say that she doesn’t like this colleague?
Rashmi: Because Madam’s tone changed one day while they were having a conversation. I went to
serve some tea when I heard Madam scream at her.
Detective: Do you know her name?
Rashmi: No, I don’t.
Detective: Anything to identify her?
Rashmi: Ummm, yes, she had a tattoo on her neck which was a foreign language.
Detective: Thank you.
